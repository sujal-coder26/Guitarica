If you want to play it using fingerpicking pattern then the pattern is:

G chord: 6-3-2-3-4-3
Cadd9 chord: 5-3-2-3-4-3
D chord: 4-3-2-3-4-3

Cadd9 chord is simple. You know the G chord. Just move your index and middle finger to the 1 step low string. This will play Cadd9. The note that you play with middle finger on 6th string will shift to the 5th string and the note on the 5th string that you play with index finger will shift to 4th
