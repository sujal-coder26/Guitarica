Why Guitarica?

Beginner Lesson

Beginner Lesson has 5 levels that will teach you all the basic stuffs and will allow you to play any simple songs. From learning the basic guitar anatomy to the chords, strumming patterns, shifting and tabs all are covered here.

Intermediate Lesson

Intermediate Lesson has 5 levels that will teach you all the Intermediate stuffs and will allow you to play Intermediate songs. From learning the fretboard to the Barre chords, fingerpicking / fingerstyling, learning intermeidate chords all are covered here.

Advance Lesson

Advance Lesson has 6 levels that will teach you the Music and Guitar theory. These theories will help guitarist to make song, understand song, understand the arrangements of the song. Only learning chords and guitar basic in not enough. One should know the basic theories of guitar and music
